We are recruiting club activity instructors for elementary schools!
From September 2021, all 262 municipal elementary schools in the 16 wards of the city will implement new after-school sports and cultural activities commissioned by the private sector.
We are looking for instructors and assistants for these activities.
You should have experience in sports or music (choir, musical instrument performance, etc.) and be able to teach children at elementary schools from 3:00 to 6:00 pm from Tuesday to Friday (training on instructing and safety management will be provided).
Instruction, training, and reporting will be conducted in Japanese.
For applications, please register at "Nagoya Club Activities Personnel Bank" <https://jinzaibank-nagoya.jp>.
New sports and cultural activities in Nagoya City
Activities will generally be on three days between Tuesday and Friday.
Activities will be for 4th to 6th grade students at Nagoya municipal elementary schools.
For each day of the week, children will choose one of two activities.
Children will be able to participate in up to three activities in one year.
The activities will be held at schools.
(Athletic field or gymnasium or school building)
Instruction will be provided by two or more instructors from the operator.
Participation is voluntary, but we invite you to take part to contribute to improving the physical strength of children and nurturing a rich sense of humanity in them.
