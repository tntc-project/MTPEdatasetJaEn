We are looking for elementary school club coaches!
From September 2021, all 262 municipal elementary schools in 16 wards of the city will have new after-school athletic and cultural activities through consignment to the private sector.
We are looking for people who will assist as coaches and in coaching and leading in the activities.
This is for those who have experience in sports and music (such as chorus and musical instruments), and who are able to coach children at elementary schools from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m. between Tuesday to Friday (who have training in coaching and safety management).
Coaching, training and reporting will be conducted in Japanese.
To apply, please register at "https://jinzaibank-nagoya.jp Club Activity human resource agency" <Nagoya>.
Nagoya City's New Athletic and Cultural Activities
The activities are fundamentally held on 3 days between Tuesday to Friday.
The target is 4th to 6th graders of Nagoya municipal elementary schools.
Each day, children choose from one of two events.
Children can participate in up to 3 events in a year.
The activities are at school.
(In athletic grounds, gymnasiums, and school buildings)
Coaching is given by two or more coaches of the management company.
Participation is voluntary, but please do so as part of improving children's physical strength and fostering a rich sense of humanity in them.
